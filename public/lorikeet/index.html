Chapter 03
Today every platform responds.
Tomorrow's will operate.

The agent detects, locates, verifies, acts with approval where consequential, and reports. The interface becomes a theatre of work, generative UI adapting per context. Sierra, Decagon ($4.5B, a16z-backed), and Intercom Fin can all take backend actions today. The differentiator is not capability. It is trust architecture: granular permissions, auditable execution, and interfaces that adapt per context. Lorikeet is positioned to define this next layer.

Signal 01 · Incumbent
Zendesk customer since 2019
Published case study on Zendesk's own site. Their Head of Customer Support describes Zendesk as a tool for "scalable human support." Infrastructure, not intelligence. Displaceable. Compare with Revolut, who just launched AIR in-house.
Source · zendesk.co.uk/customer/wise (first-party customer story)
Signal 02 · Regulatory pressure
Compliance enforcement is generating CX load
Belgium's NBB forced a remediation plan in late 2024, triggering mass account freezes. US multistate settlement of $4.2M in July 2025 over AML gaps. These generate high-consequence tickets (KYC disputes, AML reviews, cross-jurisdiction escalations) that Zendesk cannot automate.
Source · Financial Times (Nov 2024), NY DFS press release (Jul 2025)
Signal 03 · Timing
Nasdaq primary listing on May 11, 2026
Primary listing moving from LSE to Nasdaq on May 11. Wise's published FRT is 15 hours for 80% of email queries. That number does not survive US analyst scrutiny. A concierge compressing FRT to minutes is an investor-narrative asset.
Source · Finance Magnates (Apr 2026), Wise FY2026 reporting
Signal 04 · Adjacency
Airwallex is already a Lorikeet customer
Lorikeet already serves Airwallex (cross-border payments, B2B). Wise is the consumer/SMB analog. Same domain, different segment, no conflict. Deployment playbooks transfer. QED's fintech thesis maps cleanly.
Source · Lorikeet customer list (public), QED Investors portfolio thesis
How this lands
Phase 1 · Weeks 1-6
Coexist. Lorikeet handles what Zendesk cannot.
KYC disputes, account freeze queries, and AML escalations route to Lorikeet. Everything else stays in Zendesk. Wise's team sees immediate relief on their hardest tickets. No migration risk.

Phase 2 · Weeks 7-14
Expand. Concierge covers the next tier.
Transfer disputes, multi-currency questions, card issues move to Lorikeet. Playbooks from Phase 1 accelerate configuration. Zendesk handles general enquiries and routing.

Phase 3 · Steady state
Lorikeet resolves. Zendesk routes.
Lorikeet is the resolution engine across all high-value workflows. Zendesk persists as ticketing infrastructure and routing layer. No rip-and-replace. Wise keeps their investment.
